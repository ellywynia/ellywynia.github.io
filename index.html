Since 1992 I have been working with Individuals and Families specializing in a family systems modality. Presently the majority of my practice is Couples and Sex Therapy with a foundational frame of IMAGO therapy. I enjoy helping people create conscious connection within themselves and each other. I believe when people are connected with who they are intellectually, spiritually, physically and emotionally they have a better possibility of living happy full lives. As a Social Worker, I try hard to help people connect with the world around them by encouraging stretching outside of one's comfort zone to partake in all that our community and world has to offer. Our work together will help you more deeply connect with yourself and all of those you are in relationship with.

All Couples sessions are 90 minutes in length. Also offering Intensive Couples therapy for 3-6 hours in length.

Individual sessions are 45, 60 and 90 minute sessions.

Family sessions are also 90 minutes in length with offerings of 3 hour sessions as well.

SUPERVISION:
I am available for clinical supervision for people pursuing their MSW or Counseling Degrees and wanting to consult on cases or to navigate the private practice world. Please contact me for available times.
