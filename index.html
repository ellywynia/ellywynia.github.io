Since 1992 I have been working with Individuals and Families specializing in a family systems modality. Presently the majority of my practice is Couples and Sex Therapy with a foundational frame of IMAGO therapy. I enjoy helping people create conscious connection within themselves and each other. I believe when people are connected with who they are intellectually, spiritually, physically and emotionally they have a better possibility of living happy full lives. As a Social Worker, I try hard to help people connect with the world around them by encouraging stretching outside of one's comfort zone to partake in all that our community and world has to offer. Our work together will help you more deeply connect with yourself and those you are in a relationship with.

    
SESSION DETAILS:
~ All Couples sessions are 90 minutes with an offering of 3~6 hours.
~ Individual sessions are 45, 60 and 90 minutes.
